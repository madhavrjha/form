Declarations
Via the Parent/Guardian Portal – all information pertaining to a Parent/Guardian and Student can be submitted and updated including the upload of applicable documents as may be required. A Parent/Guardian can view their child’s Subject Registrations; Attendance and Academic Report Cards via their Portal. The Parent/Guardian can view work/topics that have been covered in class and documents for the topic can also be downloaded e.g.; for Units of Inquiry by subject; rubrics etc. A Parent/Guardian can view their child’s homework assignments/projects and any documents for their class/or assigned to them as group of students/or as individual students. Parents/Guardians can also view their child’s Code of Conduct and positive and negative incidents. Parents/Guardians can also view their child’s houses and Extra-Curriculum activities
